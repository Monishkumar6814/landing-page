What's in VCET ?

Velalar College Of Engineering And Technology admission 2024 for MBA and MCA programs will be done through the
TANCET examination. TANCET 2024 registrations are open and the last date to apply has been extended to February 12,
2024. Interested candidates can register online on the official website @tancet.annauniv.edu. TANCET 2024 exam for MBA and MCA programs will be held on March 09, 2024.

Velalar College of Engineering and Technology is an autonomous college, affiliated to Anna University, Tamil Nadu and approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE).
The college has been accredited 'A' Grade by NAAC. It is a private college that provides co-educational services to both the genders. 90% of the students get placements from the on-campus interview held in the college. The top recruiting companies lists include Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and many other IT companies. The only mission of VCET is to expose the students to world-class education and convey a wide range of quality knowledge to them. VCET has Ranked in 140th position for B.Tech, by Indiatoday 2020.
